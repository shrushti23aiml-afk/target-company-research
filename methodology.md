I used Google search, LinkedIn, and industrial directories to identify manufacturing companies in Bengaluru. 

I filtered companies based on:
- Manufacturing capability (not distributors)
- Industrial sensor / instrumentation segment
- MSME size range
- Website and product presence

Revenue was estimated using company size, product range, and market presence where exact data was unavailable.

Each company was evaluated using the Federer scoring model (C1–C6) with evidence-based reasoning.
